How to play

Mancala is a two-player turn-based game. On your turn, pick up all stones from one of your pits and sow them one-by-one into the following pits in a counter-clockwise direction. If the last stone lands in your store, you get another turn. Capture occurs when the last stone lands in an empty pit on your side â€” capture that stone and the stones opposite into your store.

Rules

  • Each player has six pits and a store.
  • Players alternate turns; if your last stone lands in your store, play again.
  • When one side is empty, remaining stones on the opponent's side go to their store.
  • The player with the most stones in their store at the end wins.
